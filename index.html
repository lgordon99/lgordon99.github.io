About Me

Hello! I’m Lucia, and I have been a PhD student in Computer Science at Harvard’s School for Engineering and Applied Sciences since Fall 2022. I work as a researcher in Milind Tambe’s Teamcore group. My undergraduate background is in physics and mathematics, but my graduate research interests lie in machine learning methods and applications to conservation, climate change, and sustainability. I’m currently working on an active learning + computer vision method to locate rhino middens in UAV imagery as well as improving bandit methods for effective anti-poaching patrol planning.
